Inspired by Josh Wardle's fantastic Wordle and qntm's very cool adversarial version Absurdle, in Survivle it is your goal to lose!

The game plays just like the original in (an even stricter) hardmode: All revealed information has to be used. However, the goal now is to take as many wrong guesses as possible.

Since it is very easy to lose track of the revealed information, the game will let you know what exactly (if anything) is wrong with your current guess.

Good Luck!
