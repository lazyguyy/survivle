This is yet another creation inspired by Josh Wardle's very popular Wordle.

But in contrast to the original, the goal is to make as many wrong guesses as possible. In order to make the game somewhat challenging, all revealed information must be used.

This is like Wordle hardmode, only even more restrictive (because the original Wordle does not force you to make use of all available information).

Since it is very easy to accidentally ignore information, the game will let you know what exactly (if anything) is wrong with your current guess.

Good Luck!
